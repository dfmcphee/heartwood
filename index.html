Our story

The Heartwood is run by two sisters from the Ottawa Valley. They share a vision for a place for all of the things they love — gathering people together, sharing good music, and serving delicious food.

The name Heartwood is an homage to their late father, Donald McPhee. Heartwood Guitars was his business in Rockingham where he worked as a luthier. The restaurant is named in his memory to honour not just to his craft and creativity but also his entrepreneurial spark.
