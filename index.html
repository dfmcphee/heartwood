Our story

The Heartwood is owned by two sisters from the Ottawa Valley. They share a vision for a place to do all of the things they love — gathering people together, sharing good music, and serving delicious food.

The name Heartwood is an homage to their late father, Donald McPhee, who built guitars out of his business Heartwood Guitars. The restaurant is named in his memory to honour not just his craft and creativity, but also his entrepreneurial spirit.
